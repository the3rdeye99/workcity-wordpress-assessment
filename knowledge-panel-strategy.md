# Workcity Knowledge Panel Strategy

## Overview
A Google Knowledge Panel displays key business information directly in search results. This condensed guide covers essential steps to trigger and strengthen Workcity's panel.

---

## 1. Knowledge Panel Triggers (Must-Haves)

✓ **Distinctive entity name** - "Workcity" (unique, consistent everywhere)  
✓ **Complete schema markup** - Organization, LocalBusiness, Person, Service, Website  
✓ **Verified website** - HTTPS, mobile-responsive, fast loading  
✓ **Active online presence** - Social media, Google My Business  
✓ **Multiple authoritative citations** - Business directories, local listings  

**Quick Win:** Complete Google My Business profile with all business details, high-quality images, and weekly updates.

## 3. Schema Requirements

**Implement all 5 core schema types:**

### Organization Schema
```json
{
  "@type": "Organization",
  "name": "Workcity",
  "url": "https://workcity.com",
  "logo": "https://workcity.com/logo.png",
  "description": "Professional services in Lagos, Nigeria",
  "foundingDate": "2024",
  "founder": { "name": "Workcity" },
  "address": { "streetAddress": "Lagos, Nigeria", "addressLocality": "Lagos", "addressCountry": "NG" },
  "sameAs": ["Facebook", "Instagram", "LinkedIn", "Twitter"]
}
```

### LocalBusiness Schema
- Address, phone, hours, service area, ratings

### Person Schema (Founder - Tobi Apata)
- Name, title, social profiles, company affiliation

### Service Schema
- Service names, descriptions, pricing, service area

### Website Schema
- Site name, URL, search capability

**Validation:** Test all schema at [Google Rich Results Test](https://search.google.com/test/rich-results)

## 4. Brand Identity Consistency (NAP)

**Critical for Knowledge Panel:** Name, Address, Phone must be IDENTICAL everywhere.

| Element | Requirement |
|---------|------------|
| Business Name | Exact: "Workcity" (no variations) |
| Address | Same format on all platforms |
| Phone | Same number (+234 format) |
| Website | Consistent: https://workcity.com |
| Category | "Professional Services" |

**Consistency Checklist:**
- [ ] Google My Business matches website
- [ ] Facebook info matches GMB
- [ ] Instagram bio includes phone/address
- [ ] All directory listings match
- [ ] Schema markup matches NAP

**Visual Branding:** Use identical logo, colors, and messaging across all platforms.

## 5. About Page Hierarchy

Create a comprehensive About page with these sections (in order):

```
H1: About Workcity

H2 Who We Are
- Mission statement (150 words)
- Company vision
- Core values (3-5)

H2 Our Founder
- Tobi Apata - professional photo
- Background & credentials
- LinkedIn/social links
- Founder quote

H2 Our Story
- Founding year & background
- Key milestones
- Growth trajectory

H2 Our Services
- Business consulting
- Technology services
- Digital transformation
- Enterprise solutions

H2 Team
- Team member photos
- Names, titles, brief bios

H2 Location
- Full address + map
- Service areas (Lagos coverage)
- Contact information
- Hours

H2 Credentials & Awards
- Certifications
- Industry recognition
- Affiliations
- Quality standards

H2 What Customers Say
- 3-5 testimonials
- Customer statistics
- Case study links
- Video testimonials

CTA: Contact form, phone, email, schedule button
```

**SEO Tips:**
- Title: "About Workcity | Professional Services Lagos"
- Meta: Include "Workcity," "professional services," "Lagos"
- Include founder name (Tobi Apata) & credentials
- Link to service pages and blog posts
- All images with descriptive alt text
